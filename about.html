About the Hub

The Creator

Hi, I'm Prathamesh Vattamwar, a web developer passionate about creating interactive and engaging online experiences. This Mini Game Hub started as a personal project to consolidate several small games I built while learning and experimenting with web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

My goal was to create a fun, visually appealing space where anyone could quickly jump into a simple game for a few minutes of entertainment. I focused on clean code, responsive design, and adding a touch of flair with animations using GSAP.

Our Mission

To provide a simple, accessible, and ever-growing collection of mini-games that bring a little bit of joy and challenge to your day. We believe in the power of play and the potential of the web platform to deliver delightful experiences.
